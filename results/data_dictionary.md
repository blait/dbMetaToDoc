**Domain:** healthcare / clinical (observational research)

This database is an instance of the OHDSI OMOP Common Data Model (CDM), a standardized schema for observational health research, populated with Synthea-generated synthetic patient data. It comprises three coordinated layers: (1) a standardized vocabulary layer (concept, concept_relationship, concept_ancestor, concept_synonym, vocabulary, domain, relationship, drug_strength, and lookups like concept_class) that defines and inter-relates clinical concepts drawn from terminologies such as SNOMED, RxNorm, ICD, and UCUM; (2) a clinical data layer centered on the person table (2,694 synthetic patients with demographics) and their longitudinal events â€” visits (visit_occurrence), diagnoses (condition_occurrence and aggregated condition_era), drugs (drug_exposure and aggregated drug_era), procedures (procedure_occurrence), measurements, and observations â€” all mapped to standardized concepts and tied to persons and encounters; and (3) supporting health-system, economic, and metadata structures (provider, care_site, location, cost, payer_plan_period, death, and CDM administrative tables like cdm_source and metadata). Many tables â€” including economic, device, specimen, note/NLP, and detailed visit tables â€” are present but empty, reflecting the limited scope of the synthetic source. The database's purpose is to enable standardized analytics and cohort studies over patient-level clinical histories.
